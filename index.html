How this works
  1. Read each card and sort it into Not Important, Important, or Very Important.
  2. Very Important has a maximum of 10 cards. If it gets full, move a card to Important before adding another.
  3. Once all 83 cards are sorted, you'll rank your Very Important values from 1 (most significant) to 10.
  4. Your top 5 are your core values.

There's no right or wrong — your values should be genuine to you, not short-term goals. The descriptions on each card are a guide; feel free to define them as you wish.
